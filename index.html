THE FIRE ISLAND

My visit at the Fire Island National Park a peaceful and refreshing experience that allowed me to disconnect from the busy pace of everyday life. As soon as I arrived, i noticed how calm and natural the environment felt. Walking around and seeing the wildlife felt so therapeutic and i can now see why so many people come there to escape the noise of the city. One of my most memorable experiences was exploring the natural landscapes of the park. The maritime forests were beautiful and surprisingly diverse. As I walked along the trails, I saw ifferent types of plants and wildlife that thrive well in the climate at Fire Island.
